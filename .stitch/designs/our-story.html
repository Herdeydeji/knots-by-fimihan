Our Story

"Modesty is not about hiding, but about choosing what to reveal. It is the ultimate expression of self-possession."

Fimihan's Vision

Born from the vibrant pulse of Lagos and the timeless values of Islamic tradition, Knots by Fimihan was founded to redefine the modest fashion landscape in Nigeria. Our founder, Fimihan, recognized a gap between global fashion trends and the specific needs of the modern Nigerian woman who seeks both elegance and adherence to her faith.

We believe that clothing is a silent dialogue. It speaks of where we come from and who we aspire to be. Our garments are designed to be knots—strong, intricate connections between heritage and the contemporary world.
