Frequently Asked Questions

How long does custom tailoring take? expand_more
Our bespoke pieces typically require 4 to 6 weeks for completion. This allows our artisans to meticulously hand-craft every detail, from Islamic geometric embroideries to structural finishes.
Do you offer international shipping? expand_more
Yes, we ship our Nigerian-crafted garments globally. Delivery times vary by location, typically ranging from 7 to 14 business days via our premium logistics partners.
What is your return policy for bespoke items? expand_more
Due to the highly personalized nature of bespoke garments, they are non-refundable. However, we offer complimentary adjustments within 15 days of receipt to ensure the perfect fit.
